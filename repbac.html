RepBAC

Reputation Based Access Control

Access based on your reputation.

Reputation awards are given by people and organizations to build up your reputation to solidify your DID. It uses Web of Trust by people signing your DID's attributes vouching for them. For example, you could provide a number of skills you're proficient in and people who believe you are proficient in them could sign them, something like LinkedIn supports except this is using cryptography so that it can be verified by anyone and thus not having to trust LinkedIn. Any and all attributes can be signed and revoked. Another example could be an attribute that says you're over 21 years of age. That could be checked to see the number of adults who signed it and thus how likely you are to believe the person is over 21.
